Hannah's rigatoni recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/3 cup vodka
  • enough garlic to kill a vampire
  • 1 tube of double concentrated tomato paste
  • oregano (to your heart's desire)
  • red pepper flakes (as much heat as you can handle)
  • 1 whole package of rigatoni
  • grated parmesean (to your heart's desire)
  • 1 chopped yellow onion
  • fresh chopped parsley or basil (garnish)
  • chicken or shrimp (optional, add as much as you'd like)

Steps:

  • 1. If you are adding chicken/shrimp, cook that first with olive oil and a bit of salt and pepper.
  • 2. Once cooked, add the onion to the pan with olive oil, salt, red pepper flakes, black pepper, and oregano. Cook for 3 minutes.
  • 3. Add the tomato paste and garlic and cook for 5 minutes.
  • 4. Add the vodka and heavy cream and cook for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes turn it off while cooking the pasta.
  • 5. Cook the pasta in salted water & save some pasta water before draining.
  • 6. Add the rigatoni, parmesean, and a bit of pasta water to the sauce on low heat.
  • 7. Plate the pasta and add garnish.
  • 8. Enjoy!
